# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 2025  
**Last Updated:** January 2025

Trilo ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website **thetriloapp.com**, use our mobile application, join our waitlist, connect your bank accounts, or otherwise interact with our services.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

### a. Personal Information
Information you voluntarily provide, such as:
- Name
- Email address
- Password (encrypted and hashed)
- Profile picture (optional)
- Responses to surveys or forms (e.g., test user interest)

### b. Financial Information
When you connect your bank accounts through Plaid:
- Bank account information (account names, types, balances)
- Transaction history and details
- Account routing numbers (masked, not full account numbers)
- Financial institution information

**Note:** We do not store your bank account credentials. All bank authentication is handled securely through Plaid, a bank-level secure service.

### c. Authentication and Security Data
To enhance security and protect your account:
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) data:
  - Phone numbers (stored securely, masked for display)
  - SMS verification codes and timestamps
  - MFA verification attempts and timestamps
- Authentication logs (login attempts, device information)
- Session tokens and access tokens (encrypted)
- Security-related metadata

### d. Automatically Collected Information
When you visit our site or use our app, we may automatically collect:
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device type and operating system
- Mobile device identifiers
- Pages viewed, time spent, referring URLs
- App usage data and analytics
- Crash reports and error logs

This data may be collected through cookies and similar tracking technologies.

## 2. Use of Information

We use your data to:
- Provide and maintain our website, mobile application, and services
- Process your bank account connections and transaction data
- Respond to inquiries or applications (e.g., test users)
- Communicate updates, news, or product announcements
- Analyze usage to improve our service
- Ensure account security through multi-factor authentication
- Detect and prevent fraud, security threats, or unauthorized access
- Comply with legal obligations
- Enforce our terms of service

## 3. Security Measures

Trilo implements comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

### a. Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
- Two-factor authentication is required before accessing bank account features
- SMS-based verification codes sent to your registered phone number
- Phone numbers are stored securely and masked for privacy
- Verification codes expire after 10 minutes for security

### b. Encryption
- **Encryption in Transit**: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher
- **Encryption at Rest**: All user data stored in our database (Supabase) is encrypted at rest
- **Token Security**: Plaid access tokens and authentication tokens are encrypted

### c. Access Controls
- **Row Level Security (RLS)**: Database-level security ensuring users can only access their own data
- **Authentication**: Secure authentication with password hashing
- **Session Management**: Secure session handling with automatic timeout

### d. Vulnerability Management
- Regular automated vulnerability scanning of dependencies and infrastructure
- Timely patching of security vulnerabilities
- Security-focused code reviews

### e. Third-Party Security
- We use trusted, security-compliant service providers:
  - **Supabase**: SOC 2 compliant database and authentication
  - **Plaid**: Bank-level security for financial data aggregation
  - **Railway**: Secure hosting infrastructure

## 4. Account Security

### Multi-Factor Authentication Requirement
For enhanced security, multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required before you can connect bank accounts through Plaid. This adds an extra layer of protection to your financial data.

### Your Responsibility
You are responsible for:
- Maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials
- Keeping your authenticator app secure
- Notifying us immediately of any unauthorized access
- Using strong, unique passwords

## 5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal information under one or more of the following legal bases:

- Your **consent**
- To perform a **contract** with you (providing financial management services)
- Compliance with a **legal obligation**
- Our **legitimate interests** (e.g., site security, business development, fraud prevention)

## 6. Your Rights

### a. Under GDPR (EEA users):
You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request access to your personal data
- **Rectification**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of your personal data
- **Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw consent at any time
- **Object to Processing**: Object to processing of your personal data
- **Data Portability**: Receive your data in a portable format
- **Lodge a Complaint**: Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at **privacy@thetriloapp.com**

### b. Under CCPA (California residents):
You have the right to:
- **Know**: Know what personal information we collect and how we use it
- **Delete**: Request deletion of your personal data
- **Opt Out**: Opt out of the sale of your personal information (we **do not** sell your data)
- **Non-Discrimination**: Not be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights

To make a request, email **privacy@thetriloapp.com** with the subject line **"CCPA Request"**

## 7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies or third-party tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to improve your experience and understand how visitors interact with our site.

You can adjust your cookie settings in your browser or device preferences.

## 8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to:
- Fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our legitimate interests
- Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

**Security and Authentication Data**: We may retain authentication logs and security-related data for up to 12 months for security and fraud prevention purposes.

**Financial Data**: Transaction and account data is retained while your account is active and you have connected bank accounts. When you disconnect a bank account, associated transaction data is deleted.

**Account Data**: Account information is retained while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account at any time.

## 9. Data Transfers

If you are located outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries that may not have equivalent privacy laws. We use appropriate safeguards to ensure adequate protection of your data (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses under GDPR).

## 10. Children's Privacy

Trilo does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that a child has submitted personal data, we will delete it immediately.

## 11. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party service providers to help operate our website and application (e.g., email marketing platforms, analytics tools, payment processors). These providers are contractually obligated to handle your data securely and in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

**Key Third-Party Services:**
- **Plaid**: For secure bank account connections and transaction data
- **Supabase**: For database and authentication services
- **Railway**: For application hosting
- **RevenueCat**: For subscription management
